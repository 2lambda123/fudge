Downloading
===========

The latest version of Fudge and GNDS is available from https://github.com/LLNL/fudge.
Users with LLNL gitlab accounts can also access FUDGE at https://lc.llnl.gov/gitlab/nuclear/fudge.

Please `let us know <mailto:mattoon1@llnl.gov>`_ if you have any difficulty accessing the code!
